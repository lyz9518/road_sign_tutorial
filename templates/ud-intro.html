Introduction

The written test for a Class D, M, DJ, MJ or E license examines knowledge of the rules of the road, safe driving techniques, road signs and the laws about alcohol and drug use while driving. To pass the written test, you must correctly answer at least 14 of the 20 questions asked, but you must correctly answer two of the four questions about road signs.

This tutorial will help you better understand and recognize the road signs in NY state.

**The quizzes followed the tutorials can only be used for practice purpose. The official test is issued and regulated by the New York Department of Motor Vehicles
